About

The Claremont Journal of Medicine and Social Justice is a 5C organization formed to raise awareness around both global and local intersectional issues in medicine and public health in a socially conscious way. Articles will be published both digitally on our website and in a biannual print edition. All 5C students are welcome to be a team member. The current positions we are looking for interested students to fill: global content editor, global content writer, local content editor, local content writer, and finance team member. There will be full team meetings every other Wednesday from 9-10 at SOCA Lounge. If you are interested in joining the team or learning more about the journal please contact info.cjmsj@gmail.com.
